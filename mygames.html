Millport Camp is a single-player, open-world, third-person zombie shooter game.
Description
You are Harley, just on your way to meet up with your friend in the north in a zombie apocalypse. Who knows what will happen?

Main Achievements

  • Various game/design patterns for gameplay features and UI: game loop, observer, singleton, flyweight, prototype, and MVP (Model, View, and Presenter).
  • A simple yet functional finite state machine (HFSM) for AI behaviors.

Dead Island | C#, Unity
March 2024 - April 2024
Dead Island is an interactive agents and procedural generation demo game.
Description
A squad of AI survivors tries to navigate through some ocean islands populated with wandering AI zombies to reach a safe zone.
Highlights
AI agents operate using behavior trees (BTs) powered by NPBehave, with squad coordination among survivors inspired by Days Gone. Islands are procedurally generated with binary space partitioning and cellular automata.
